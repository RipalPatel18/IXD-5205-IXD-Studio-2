**How can voice-activated technologies (tablets, phones, VR, or smart devices like Alexa/Google Home) support individuals aging at home to maintain and enhance social connections, emotional expression, and communication?**

## üè° Project Overview
This project explores how conversational voice-based technology can help older adults live independently at home while staying emotionally and socially connected.  
We designed a **human-centered voice assistant** concept that balances **privacy, accessibility, empathy, and trust** ‚Äî supporting daily routines, communication, and emotional well-being.

---

## üéØ Design Goals
1. **Accessibility & Ease of Use:**  
   Simplify navigation through voice commands and intuitive feedback.  
   Ensure interaction feels natural and friendly for non-tech-savvy users.

2. **Emotional Sensitivity:**  
   Detect tone, frustration, or loneliness and respond empathetically.  
   Use voice tone modulation and supportive prompts.

3. **Cultural & Linguistic Diversity:**  
   Support multiple languages or familiar voice options (e.g., a family member‚Äôs voice).

4. **Privacy vs. Connection:**  
   Maintain user control over what data is shared while offering meaningful interaction.

5. **Physical + Virtual Integration:**  
   Combine virtual feedback with subtle physical cues (light, sound, or haptics).

## üß© Prototype Concept
### **Zen-Care: Voice Assistant for Older Adults**
Zen-Care is a prototype designed to simulate **empathetic daily interactions**:
- Offers personalized voice prompts and emotional responses.  
- Connects users to family and caregivers through simple voice phrases.  
- Tracks basic routines (hydration, medication, mood).  
- Demonstrates tone recognition and empathetic feedback through scripted interactions.

---

## üß† Research Insights
- Older adults prefer **gentle, conversational tones** over robotic ones.  
- **Predictable daily cues** foster trust and reduce anxiety.  
- **Large, forgiving UI** elements and clear voice feedback improve accessibility.  
- **Family connection** remains a central motivator for adopting new tech.
